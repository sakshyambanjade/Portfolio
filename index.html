Research

research

Featured research, papers, and research interests centered on LLM evaluation, applied AI systems, and scientific tooling.

I am interested in how AI systems behave under real constraints: limited data, messy deployment environments, and evaluation setups that can mislead us if we are not careful.

My current research questions sit around LLM reasoning, evaluation design, applied AI systems for real users, and how to build research that stays useful outside the lab.

selected work

A focused view of the AI systems, machine learning systems, applied AI products, and Nepal-focused technology systems I have built or contributed to.

2026

SeedSure AI

SeedSure AI is an AI-powered climate-smart seed and crop advisory platform focused on Nepal's smallholder farmers. It helps farmers make better seed and crop decisions using location, weather, season, soil type, and local farming conditions. The project was selected among the top 6 out of 50+ pitches at UNDP Nepal.

2026

FastNewsOrg

FastNewsOrg is an AI-powered news aggregation, curation, summarization, and automated publishing system for politics, finance, technology news, and election monitoring workflows.

2025

MolVis Pro

Browser-based molecular visualization and AI-assisted chemistry platform built around SMILES input, molecular properties, descriptors, visualization, and accessible scientific workflows.

Multi-AI Orchestrator

Unified system for coordinating multiple language models and providers, exploring routing, consensus, benchmarking, structured outputs, and parallel reasoning.
